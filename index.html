Research Profile

I am a groundwater researcher specializing in fractured aquifer characterization using an integrated framework of laboratory flow-cell experiments, field-scale hydraulic and tracer tests, inverse modeling, and physics-informed machine learning.

My research focuses on developing information-driven and scalable subsurface imaging techniques to improve aquifer characterization in complex geologic systems. The outcomes of my work directly support groundwater sustainability and decision support by aligning process-based modeling with data-driven approaches in hydrogeology.
